\block{Introduction}{The aim of WWFS-FWD'2016 is to encourage the participation of women in free software (FLOSS) projects in various capacities, to empower them, to deliberate on related gender issues and  introduce cutting edge free software. Fedora Women's Day celebrations will be part of the workshop on the first day of the workshop.} 

\block{Organizations Involved}{The two day workshop is organized by 
\begin{itemize}
 \item{Ubuntu Women Project,}
 \item{Fedora Project (Fedora Women) and}
 \item{Netaji Subhash Engg, College, Kolkata}
\end{itemize}}
\block{When and Where?}{ 
\begin{itemize}
 \item{On 15th and 16th of July'2016}
 \item{Time: 11:00 to 17:00 hrs.}
 \item{Venue: Netaji Subhash Engg College\\
   Garia, Kolkata}
\end{itemize}}

\block{What will Participants Gain?}{
Partcipants would be able to
\begin{itemize}
 \item {use new free software, understand FLOSS development environments and opportunities,}
 \item {specifically be able to contribute to Fedora and Ubuntu development without any apprehensions}
 \item {create inclusive workspaces,}
 \item {set discriminatory and sexist workspaces in order and}
 \item {navigate their future workspaces in a seamless way.}
\end{itemize}}
